CHORUS

Let me go home. Thy part and mine to-day Will best be borne, if thou obey me in that.

JOCASTA

Disloyal and ungrateful! to deprive The state that reared thee of thine utterance now

CHORUS

Thy speech, I see, is foiling thine intent; And I would shield me from the like mishap.

JOCASTA

Nay, if thou knowest, turn thee not away: All here with suppliant hands importune thee.
